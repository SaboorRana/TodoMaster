About This App

This To-Do web app is designed to help you stay organized and manage your daily tasks efficiently. Whether you’re planning your day, tracking a project, or setting personal goals, this app provides a simple and user-friendly interface to create, update, and delete tasks effortlessly.

Built using Flask, this app demonstrates the power of Python in web development, leveraging templates for dynamic content and a database to store and manage your tasks. It’s perfect for students, professionals, or anyone who wants to take control of their productivity.
